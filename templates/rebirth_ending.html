The Rebirth of the Archivist

The final fragment falls into place.

Memories surge through the void.

Before the halls of endless tomes, before the chains of duty — he was human.

Love, betrayal, hope, despair... each a flame rekindled in the darkness.

The Library shudders.

You see him now: not as a warden of the forgotten, but as a soul reborn.

The past, once shattered, is whole again.

The Archivist smiles — and somewhere, the first star is born anew.
